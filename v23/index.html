YUNNAN

In-Flight Destination Guide

Seven cities. One province. Infinite altitude.

Presented by Yunnan Airways

昆明

THE SPRING CITY

WHERE STONE FORESTS RISE FROM RED EARTH

@YunnanExplorer

KUNMING

The Spring City. 15 degrees year-round. The Stone Forest outside town looks like a lithified ocean -- 270-million-year-old limestone karsts towering above red earth paths. Flowers bloom in every season here.

2.3k #SpringCity

大理

BETWEEN MOUNTAIN AND WATER

THREE PAGODAS GUARD THE ANCIENT KINGDOM

@DaliDrifter

DALI

Bai architecture lines the shores of Erhai Lake. Cangshan Mountain rises 4,000 meters behind the old town. The marble here has been famous for a thousand years -- the Chinese word for marble literally means "Dali stone."

4.1k #ErhaiBreezes

丽江

A LABYRINTH OF CANALS

NAXI TRADITIONS WOVEN IN WATER AND STONE

@OldTownWanderer

LIJIANG

UNESCO World Heritage since 1997. The old town has no city walls -- the Naxi rulers said walls would box in their name (the character for their surname inside a box means "trapped"). Canals still run clean from Black Dragon Pool.

3.7k #NaxiHeritage

玉龙雪山

THE JADE DRAGON

5,596 METERS OF UNCLIMBED SNOW

虎跳峡

THE GORGE THAT SWALLOWS SOUND

3,900 METERS FROM RIVER TO RIDGE

@GorgeHiker

TIGER LEAPING GORGE

One of the deepest gorges on the planet. The Jinsha River roars below while Jade Dragon and Haba Snow Mountains flank each side. A tiger once leapt across the narrowest point -- or so the story goes. The two-day hike is legendary.

5.8k #GorgeTrail

香格里拉

WHERE THE HIMALAYAS BEGIN

SONGZANLIN -- THE LITTLE POTALA

@ShangriLaNomad

SHANGRI-LA

Formerly Zhongdian, renamed after James Hilton's fictional paradise. At 3,160 meters, the air is thin and the monasteries are gilded. Songzanlin houses 700 monks and mirrors the Potala Palace in miniature. Yak butter tea is the local fuel.

3.2k #LostHorizon

元阳

THIRTEEN CENTURIES OF PATIENCE

HANI TERRACES CARVED INTO CLOUD AND WATER

@TerraceChaser

YUANYANG

The Hani people have sculpted these terraces for 1,300 years. At sunrise, flooded paddies become mirrors reflecting the sky in ribbons down the mountainside. UNESCO inscribed them in 2013. Best visited from November to April when the water catches light.

6.4k #RiceTerraces
@YunnanFoodie

YUNNAN FOOD GUIDE

Crossing-the-Bridge Noodles -- Kunming's signature. Assemble at the table.

Erkuai -- Dali rice cakes, grilled or stir-fried.

Wild Mushroom Hotpot -- Seasonal. Worth the risk.

Rushan -- Fan-shaped fried goat cheese, only in Dali.

Yak Butter Tea -- Shangri-La essential. Salty, warming.

Steam Pot Chicken -- Slow-cooked in a Jianshui clay pot.

8.9k #YunnanEats
