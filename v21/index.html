昆明Kunming -- The Spring City

Eternal spring at 1,900 metres. Flowers bloom year-round in a city that never quite feels like it is in a hurry. The gateway to Yunnan -- most journeys begin here.

春

15C year-round. Locals call it the city of eternal spring -- bring a light jacket, never a heavy coat.

温

The Stone Forest: 270 million years of limestone karst, standing like a petrified army. UNESCO World Heritage since 2007.

石
Lake views and ancient pagodas

The Three Pagodas of Chongsheng Temple, standing since the 9th century against the Cangshan mountain backdrop. A thousand years of silence beside Erhai Lake.

@YunnanTravel 2.4k

大理Dali -- Wind, Flowers, Snow, Moon

Erhai Lake stretches 250 square kilometres, ringed by the snow-dusted peaks of Cangshan. The Bai people have lived here for centuries, their architecture a poetry of white walls and ink-painted eaves.

风

Rushan cheese: Bai milk fans, stretched by hand and grilled crisp. Best eaten warm from the street vendor's charcoal grill.

食

丽江Lijiang -- Naxi Heartland

A labyrinth of canals and dark-tiled roofs. 800 years of Naxi civilization preserved in narrow alleys, where Dongba script still decorates the lintels and the old orchestra plays music lost elsewhere in China.

古

玉龙雪山Jade Dragon Snow Mountain

5,596 metres of ice and legend, never summited. The Naxi believe it is the dwelling place of their protective god, Sanduo. Its thirteen peaks resemble a dragon in flight.

龙

Naxi Ancient Music: an orchestra of octogenarians playing Tang dynasty compositions. Hear it in the Old Town -- it may not survive another generation.

乐
Where the tiger leapt across the abyss

The Jinsha River carves between Jade Dragon and Haba Snow Mountains, nearly 4,000 metres below the peaks. One of the deepest gorges on earth -- and one of the most spectacular two-day hikes in Asia.

@GorgeHiker 5.1k

虎跳峡Tiger Leaping Gorge

Two days of trail carved into cliff face. The high path is the reward: vertiginous views, waterfalls crossing the trail, and the roar of the Jinsha far below.

虎

Pack light, bring good boots. The 28 Bends will test your knees. Guesthouses along the trail serve hot meals and cold beer.

行

香格里拉Shangri-La -- The Horizon

At 3,160 metres, the air thins and the light turns golden. Tibetan prayer flags snap in the wind above Dukezong Old Town. The name itself is borrowed from a Western novel -- but the place is entirely its own.

藏

松赞林寺Songzanlin Monastery

The "Little Potala Palace," founded in 1679 by the Fifth Dalai Lama. 700 monks still reside here. Butter lamps flicker in halls fragrant with juniper incense.

寺

Altitude warning: 3,160m. Take it slow for the first day. Drink butter tea -- the locals swear by it for acclimatization.

高
1,300 years of terraced water mirrors

The Hani people carved these terraces into the mountainside over 1,300 years. In winter, flooded paddies become mirrors reflecting sky and cloud -- a living landscape painting.

@TerraceDreamer 3.8k

元阳Yuanyang Rice Terraces

UNESCO World Heritage. The Hani's masterwork -- gravity-fed irrigation flowing from mountaintop forests through villages to terraced paddies and back again as mist. A closed-loop system older than most civilizations.

田

Sunrise at Duoyishu, 6:30am in winter. Arrive early -- the light changes fast and the terraces flood with gold for only minutes.

晨

过桥米线Crossing the Bridge Noodles

Kunming's signature dish. A boiling bowl of broth, raw ingredients added tableside. The oil layer keeps the soup scalding hot.

面

饵丝Er Si Rice Noodles

Chewy rice noodles fried or in soup. A Yunnan breakfast staple, found at every street corner from Kunming to Dali.

饵

野生菌Wild Mushrooms

Yunnan produces most of China's wild mushrooms. Summer is season -- matsutake, porcini, and dozens of unnamed species. Eaten in hot pot or stir-fried.

菌

普洱茶Pu'er Tea

Aged, fermented, and deeply earthy. The ancient tea horse road ran through Yunnan's mountains for centuries. Buy direct from Menghai or Yiwu.

茶

Route

Kunming -> Dali (5h train) -> Lijiang (2h bus) -> Tiger Leaping Gorge (3h bus) -> Shangri-La (4h bus). Yuanyang is a separate southern loop from Kunming (6h bus).

路

Best Season

March-May or September-November. Summer monsoon brings mushrooms but also mud. Winter is dry and clear -- ideal for Yuanyang terraces.

季

Altitude

Kunming 1,900m. Lijiang 2,400m. Shangri-La 3,160m. Ascend gradually. Drink water. Altitude sickness hits fast above 3,000m.
