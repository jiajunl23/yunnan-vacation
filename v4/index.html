01

KUNMING & STONE FOREST

Must Visit

Kunming is your gateway drug to Yunnan. Eternal spring weather, Green Lake Park vibes, and the legendary Stone Forest just 90 minutes out. The Shilin karst formations are 270 million years old -- older than most things you can name. Walk through towering limestone pillars that look like they belong on another planet.

15C-24CYEAR-ROUND
90 MINFROM CITY
UNESCOLISTED
02

DALI

Foodie Alert

Dali is the chill counterpart to Lijiang. Erhai Lake stretches out beneath the Cangshan mountains, and the old town keeps it real with Bai minority architecture, tie-dye workshops, and the Three Pagodas standing guard for over a thousand years. The local food scene -- rushan cheese, erkuai rice cakes, sour fish soup -- is legendary.

1,972MALTITUDE
3 PAGODASICONIC
1000+YRS HISTORY
03

LIJIANG

Must Visit

Lijiang Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage maze of canals, bridges, and Naxi Dongba culture. But the real showstopper is Jade Dragon Snow Mountain at 5,596m, visible from everywhere in town. Take the cable car up to 4,506m and feel your lungs question your life choices. Worth every gasp.

800+YRS OLD
5,596MPEAK
UNESCOHERITAGE
04

TIGER LEAPING GORGE

Adventure

One of the deepest gorges on the planet. The high trail takes two days and rewards you with vertigo-inducing views of the Jinsha River raging 3,900m below Haba Snow Mountain. The 28 bends will test your calves and your commitment. Guesthouses along the way serve cold beer and hot meals. Absolute bucket list material.

22 KMTRAIL
2 DAYSTREK
3,900MDEPTH
05

SHANGRI-LA

Hidden Gem

Formerly Zhongdian, renamed after the fictional paradise in "Lost Horizon." The Songzanlin Monastery is a mini Potala Palace. Dukezong Old Town is the highest ancient town in China. Spin the giant prayer wheel, drink yak butter tea, and feel the altitude hit different at 3,160m. Best visited September-November for golden landscapes.

3,160MALTITUDE
300+YRS OLD
SEP-NOVBEST TIME
06

YUANYANG RICE TERRACES

Hidden Gem

The Hani people carved these terraces into the mountainside over 1,300 years, creating a landscape that looks like it was designed by an algorithm for maximum beauty. At sunrise, the flooded paddies turn into mirrors reflecting clouds and sky. November to March is prime season when the terraces are filled with water. Bring a proper camera.

1,300+YRS OLD
NOV-MARBEST TIME
UNESCOLISTED

FOODIE GUIDE

THE FOOD ALONE IS WORTH THE FLIGHT

Foodie Alert

Crossing Bridge Noodles

Kunming's legendary rice noodle soup. You build it yourself at the table -- raw meat, vegetables, noodles into scalding broth. Interactive dining before it was trendy.

KUNMING
Must Try

Rushan (Milk Fan Cheese)

Fried goat milk cheese on a stick, drizzled with rose syrup. Sounds weird. Tastes incredible. Dali invented street food fusion before anyone else.

DALI
Local Fave

Baba (Naxi Flatbread)

Crispy, flaky, stuffed with savory or sweet fillings. The Naxi people perfected portable food centuries before grab-and-go was a concept.

LIJIANG
Adventure

Yak Butter Tea

Salty, buttery, polarizing. You either love it or you learn to love it. Essential fuel at 3,160m altitude in Shangri-La. No negotiation.

SHANGRI-LA
Foodie Alert

Wild Mushroom Hotpot

Yunnan is the mushroom capital of China. During rainy season, restaurants serve hotpots with 20+ varieties of wild fungi. Flavor explosion does not begin to cover it.

ALL YUNNAN
Local Fave

Erkuai (Rice Cakes)

Grilled, stir-fried, or in soup. These chewy rice cakes are Yunnan's carb of choice. Simple, satisfying, and absurdly addictive.

QUICK TIPS

SAVE THIS FOR YOUR TRIP

01

BEST TIME TO GO

March to May and September to November. Avoid Chinese National Holiday week (early October) unless you love crowds.

02

ALTITUDE IS REAL

Shangri-La sits at 3,160m. Lijiang at 2,400m. Take it slow the first day. Drink water. Skip the baijiu on night one.

03

GET THE HIGH-SPEED RAIL

Kunming to Dali in 2 hours. Kunming to Lijiang in 3.5 hours. Cheap, fast, scenic. Book on 12306 app or Trip.com.

04

CASH IS STILL USEFUL

WeChat Pay rules China, but rural areas and small vendors sometimes want cash. Keep some RMB on hand.

05

LAYER UP

Yunnan weather changes fast. Sunny morning, cold afternoon, rain by evening. A packable jacket is non-negotiable.

06

LEARN THREE PHRASES

Ni hao (hello), xie xie (thanks), duo shao qian (how much). That gets you surprisingly far in the smaller towns.
