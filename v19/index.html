THE INCREDIBLE YUNNAN

A COMIC BOOK VACATION GUIDE
6 DESTINATIONS OF WONDER!

CHAPTER 1: KUNMING -- THE SPRING CITY

OUR ADVENTURE BEGINS IN THE SPRING CITY...

KUNMING, CAPITAL OF YUNNAN PROVINCE, SITS AT 1,900 METERS AND ENJOYS SPRING-LIKE WEATHER ALL YEAR ROUND.

HOME TO THE UNESCO-LISTED STONE FOREST, WHERE TOWERING LIMESTONE PILLARS RISE FROM THE EARTH LIKE AN ARMY OF GIANTS.

THESE ROCKS ARE 270 MILLION YEARS OLD! THE SANI PEOPLE CALL THEM SHILIN -- A FOREST OF STONE!
ANCIENT!

CHAPTER 2: DALI -- LAND OF THE BAI

THE THREE PAGODAS STAND GUARD SINCE 836 AD...
MEANWHILE, IN DALI...

THE BAI PEOPLE HAVE CALLED DALI HOME FOR OVER 3,000 YEARS. THEIR ANCIENT KINGDOM OF NANZHAO ONCE RIVALED THE TANG DYNASTY.

TODAY DALI IS A HAVEN FOR ARTISTS, DREAMERS, AND THOSE WHO SEEK THE PACE OF AN OLDER WORLD.

ERHAI LAKE STRETCHES BENEATH THE CANGSHAN PEAKS...
YOU MUST TRY RUSHAN CHEESE -- THE BAI STRETCH IT THIN AND GRILL IT UNTIL GOLDEN. NOTHING ELSE LIKE IT IN ALL OF CHINA!
DELICIOUS!

CHAPTER 3: LIJIANG -- THE NAXI KINGDOM

THE ANCIENT TOWN REVEALS ITS SECRETS...
JADE DRAGON SNOW MOUNTAIN -- 5,596 METERS OF MAJESTY
THE NAXI PEOPLE STILL USE DONGBA SCRIPT -- THE ONLY LIVING PICTOGRAPHIC WRITING SYSTEM ON EARTH!
MYSTERIOUS!

CHAPTER 4: TIGER LEAPING GORGE

WHOOOOSH!
WHERE THE JINSHA RIVER ROARS THROUGH THE MOUNTAINS...

THE TWO-DAY HIGH TRAIL HIKE CLIMBS TO 2,670 METERS, WITH SHEER DROPS OF 3,000 METERS TO THE RIVER BELOW.

LEGEND SAYS A TIGER ONCE LEAPED ACROSS THE NARROWEST POINT -- JUST 25 METERS WIDE.

ONE OF THE DEEPEST CANYONS ON EARTH! THE WALLS TOWER 3,790 METERS ABOVE THE RIVER!
EXTREME!

CHAPTER 5: SHANGRI-LA -- THE LOST HORIZON

SONGZANLIN MONASTERY -- THE LITTLE POTALA PALACE
AT THE EDGE OF THE TIBETAN PLATEAU...

AT 3,160 METERS, SHANGRI-LA SITS WHERE YUNNAN MEETS TIBET. THE AIR IS THIN. THE MONASTERIES ARE VAST. THE PRAYER FLAGS SNAP IN THE WIND.

THIS IS WHERE JAMES HILTON IMAGINED HIS PARADISE -- AND THE REALITY IS NO LESS EXTRAORDINARY.

YAK BUTTER TEA... IT TASTES LIKE NOTHING I HAVE EVER EXPERIENCED. SALTY, RICH, WARMING FROM THE INSIDE OUT...
TRANSCENDENT!

CHAPTER 6: YUANYANG -- THE TERRACED MASTERPIECE

THE GRAND FINALE...

FOR 1,300 YEARS, THE HANI PEOPLE HAVE SCULPTED THESE MOUNTAINS INTO STAIRWAYS OF WATER AND RICE.

THE UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE TERRACES CASCADE FROM 2,000 METERS DOWN TO 600 METERS -- A MASTERWORK OF HUMAN DETERMINATION.

SPECTACULAR!
AT SUNRISE, THE FLOODED TERRACES TURN INTO MIRRORS OF GOLD AND CRIMSON. YOU WILL NEVER FORGET THIS SIGHT.

BONUS: THE YUNNAN FOOD FILES

SLURP!

CROSSING THE BRIDGE NOODLES

A STEAMING BOWL OF RICH CHICKEN BROTH ARRIVES WITH RAW INGREDIENTS -- PAPER-THIN MEAT, QUAIL EGGS, VEGETABLES -- THAT COOK INSTANTLY IN THE PIPING HOT SOUP.

SIZZLE!

ERKUAI RICE CAKES

CHEWY RICE CAKES GRILLED OVER CHARCOAL AND SLATHERED WITH CHILI SAUCE, FERMENTED BEAN PASTE, OR WRAPPED AROUND A FRIED DOUGH STICK.

WOW!

WILD MUSHROOM HOTPOT

YUNNAN IS THE MUSHROOM CAPITAL OF CHINA. DOZENS OF WILD VARIETIES SIMMER IN A FRAGRANT BROTH -- PORCINI, MATSUTAKE, CHANTERELLE.

YUM!

PU'ER TEA

FERMENTED AND AGED LIKE FINE WINE, PU'ER TEA FROM YUNNAN'S ANCIENT TEA FORESTS IMPROVES WITH DECADES OF STORAGE. EARTHY, SMOOTH, LEGENDARY.
