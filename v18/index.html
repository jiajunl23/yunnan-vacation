YUNNAN TRIP PLAN!!

Summer 2026
← back to gallery

1. KUNMING - Spring City!

Capital of Yunnan and gateway to the province. Known as the "Spring City" for its perfect year-round climate. Start here to adjust and explore the incredible Stone Forest.

Stone Forest (Shilin)

Fun fact: 15C year-round! No heating or AC needed.

FOOD TO TRY!!

  • Cross-bridge noodles
  • Steam pot chicken
  • Wild mushrooms (seasonal!)
  • Pu'er tea
  • Rushan (fan cheese)
  • Erkuai (rice cake)

2. DALI - Lake + Mountains

Ancient walled city nestled between Cangshan Mountains and Erhai Lake. The Bai people's culture shines through in architecture, tie-dye fabric, and incredible local cheese. Rent a bike and ride the lake shore.

Three Pagodas
Erhai Lake & Cangshan

Don't miss: Rushan cheese!! The Bai people's special grilled milk fan -- tastes incredible with rose jam.

ROUTE

Kunming → Dali → Lijiang → TLG → Shangri-La → (back to KM) → Yuanyang

~2-3 weeks total to do it right

3. LIJIANG - UNESCO Old Town

Cobblestone lanes, canals, and the mighty Jade Dragon Snow Mountain looming above. A UNESCO World Heritage site and home to the Naxi people. Get lost in the old town at night.

Lijiang Old Town
Jade Dragon Snow Mtn

Naxi music at night! Traditional orchestras play in the old town squares. Don't miss it.

4. TIGER LEAPING GORGE

One of the deepest gorges in the world. The high trail is a 2-day hike with jaw-dropping views of the Jinsha River thousands of meters below. Not for the faint-hearted!

Tiger Leaping Gorge

Pack: good shoes, rain jacket, snacks, headlamp, altitude pills

5. SHANGRI-LA

Tibetan Buddhist culture at 3,160m elevation. The massive Songzanlin Monastery is often called "Little Potala Palace." Yak butter tea, prayer flags, and thin mountain air.

Songzanlin Monastery

Altitude: 3,160m -- take it slow! Drink water, rest on arrival, no running.

6. YUANYANG TERRACES

The Hani people carved these staggering rice terraces into the hillsides over 1,300 years. When flooded and reflecting the sky at sunrise, it's one of the most beautiful landscapes on Earth.

Yuanyang Rice Terraces

Best Nov-April, sunrise at Duoyishu viewpoint. Get there before 6am!

TRAVEL TIPS

  • Best time: Mar-May or Sep-Nov
  • WeChat Pay everywhere
  • High-speed rail KM to Dali ~2hrs
  • KM to LJ by train ~3hrs
  • Bring layers -- weather varies by altitude
  • Learn a few Mandarin phrases
