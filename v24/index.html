石

Kunming. Spring City.

15 degrees year-round. Stone Forest -- 270M-year-old limestone pinnacles, Sani people call it the first wonder of the world.

TRAVEL TIP

Getting to Stone Forest: 90 min by bus from Kunming East Station. Go early -- tour groups flood in by 10am. The 'minor' stone forest is quieter and just as dramatic.

塔

Dali. Three Pagodas since the 9th century.

Erhai Lake 250km of water. Cangshan 4,000m. Bai rushan cheese.

SIDE NOTE

Erhai by Bike: Rent an e-bike and circle the lake. 130km of flat shoreline road, villages every few km, fish grilled on sticks at every stop.

纳

No walls -- Naxi rulers said walls box in their name.

Dongba pictographic script. Tang-dynasty orchestras at night.

LOCAL SECRET

Skip Lijiang crowds, go to Shuhe. 4km north, same canals and cobblestones, a tenth of the tourists. Leather workshops and quiet tea houses.

龙

5,596m, never summited.

Blue Moon Valley turquoise lakes. Southernmost glacier in the Northern Hemisphere.

HEALTH NOTE

Altitude awareness: the cable car takes you to 4,506m. Bring oxygen canisters (sold at base), move slowly, skip if you have heart or lung conditions.

虎

Deepest gorge on Earth.

Misty mornings, river roaring a vertical mile below. 28 Bends. 2-day high trail.

PACKING LIST

Gorge essentials: sturdy boots, rain shell, 2L water, headlamp, snacks. Guesthouses have beds and beer but no gear shops.

寺

3,160m. 700 monks.

Golden roofs under plateau sunlight. 146 steps. Yak butter tea.

FOOD TIP

Yak everything in Shangri-La: yak butter tea, yak meat hotpot, yak yogurt. The dried yak jerky is surprisingly good trail food.

田

60 generations of Hani farming.

Duoyishu sunrise. Paradise on earth. UNESCO 2013.

TIMING TIP

Yuanyang sunrise protocol: arrive at Duoyishu by 5:30am. Terraces flood Nov-Mar, mirror the sky. Bring a tripod. Leave by 8am before haze.

FOOD GUIDE

Must-eat Yunnan dishes: crossing-the-bridge noodles, erkuai rice cakes, wild mushroom hotpot, Dali grilled fish, pu'er tea everything, rose petal pastries.

PRACTICAL

Know before you go: Yunnan is +6hrs GMT, no daylight saving. Mandarin + local languages. Altitude varies 500m to 5,500m. Dry season Nov-Apr. Rainy Jun-Sep.
